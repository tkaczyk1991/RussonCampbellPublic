Athena Way

Athena Way is a stunning new development, consisting of just 5 houses, with a mixture of 4-bed Detached and 3-bed link-detached homes. The two larger properties boast double garages and generous parking space. For the smaller family homes there are contemporary car ports.

The Local Area

Athena Way boasts an ideal location, being only a 5-minute walk from Oundleâ€™s town centre, and an even shorter distance to the local Waitrose. It is also adjacent to Laxton Junior School, the independent co-educational day school.

Oundle, Northamptonshire

Oundle is an historic market town, on the River Nene in Northamptonshire, famous for its Georgian Streetscapes and impressive limestone buildings. It is home to a range of independent shops, coffee houses, pubs and eateries, and of course the famous public school.

Transport Links

Upon leaving Athena Way, it is an extremely easy route out of the town, with a 20 minute drive to the A1 motorway, and a 25 minute drive to Peterborough train station where you can catch trains directly to London, Leeds, Birmingham and Edinburgh.
