About Us
 Russon Campbell Developments

Collin Russon
- Director

Collin Russon, Bespoke Builder and Stonework Specialist, has completed a varied and impressive range of building projects over his career, ranging from bespoke new builds to multi-storey office/apartment blocks. He has considerable knowledge of innovative building methods, techniques and materials. He has experience of working on both green and brown field sites, and also on contaminated land.

Tom Campbell
- Director

Tom Campbell was inspired by his family heritage of building houses, schools and pubs in the town of Hyde, Cheshire nearly a hundred years ago. He completed a Real estate degree at Oxford Brookes and then started his career in the residential housing sector with a small portfolio of buy-to-let houses which he renovated by himself and with carefully identified tradesmen. Through his partnership with Collin he is expanding his knowledge and experience in the construction industry and is ambitious to build Russon Campbell Developments into a significant regional developer.

Jenny Campbell
- Director

Behind the scenes Jenny is using her considerable experience as a banker, businesswoman and entrepreneur to manage the financial side of the business, as well as the Sales and Marketing aspects. Building the development to time, cost and quality targets are her key principles, as well as creating a trouble-free and enjoyable buying experience for the customer.

Andy Campbell
- Director

Andy has a career background as an engineer, and some ten years in procurement. His obsessive eye for detail, interest in kitchen and bathroom design, lighting and innovative technologies are used in an overview capacity within the Company.
